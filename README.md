# Sales Data Analysis

This project analyzes a sales dataset to uncover meaningful business insights. The goal is to understand sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends using Python-based analysis.

---

## Project Overview
The analysis includes:
- Revenue & profit trends  
- Top-selling products and categories  
- Region-wise sales performance  
- Customer purchase behavior  
- KPI calculation & business insights  
- Visualizations for data storytelling  

---

## Technologies & Libraries Used
- **Python**
- **Pandas**
- **NumPy**
- **Matplotlib**
- **Jupyter Notebook**

---

## Key Insights
- Identification of high-performing products
- Seasonal effects on sales
- Region-wise profit contribution
- Customer trends & purchase frequency
- Clean visual breakdowns supporting business strategy
